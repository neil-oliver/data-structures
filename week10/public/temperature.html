Is It Cold In Here?

The New York City Heat Law states that from October 1 to May 31 Property owners must provide tenants with heat under the following conditions: Between the hours of 6 a.m. and 10 p.m., if the outside temperature falls below 55 degrees, the inside temperature is required to be at least 68 degrees Fahrenheit; and, Between the hours of 10 p.m. and 6 a.m., the inside temperature is required to be at least 62 degrees Fahrenheit.
